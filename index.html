The secret password to the treasure is a non-repeating four-digit code. You have 10 guesses to crack the code and find the treasure.

You get two kinds of feedback for your guesses:
A: correct digit in the correct spot. Way to go!
B: correct digit in an incorrect spot. You are so close!

After 10 wrong guesses, the treasure will self-destruct and you lose the treasure.

Good Luck!
