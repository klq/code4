The secret password to the treasure is a non-repeating four digit code.
You have 10 guesses to crack the code and find the treasure.

Here's some help:
After each guess, the lock will tell you how well you did.
A: correct digit in the correct spot. Way to go!
B: correct digit in an incorrect spot. You are so close.

After 10 wrong guesses, the vault will self-destruct and you lose the treasure.

Good Luck!
